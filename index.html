Chocolate Brownies

A chocolate brownie is a baked confection.
It can come in different forms and may either be fudgy or cakey depending on their density.
Typically, a brownie may include ingredients such as; nuts, frosting and chocolate chips.

Ingredients List

Ingredients Amount
Sugar 2.0 Cups
Flour 1.5 Cups
Butter 1.0 Cup
Eggs 4.0 Pieces
Cocoa Powder 0.5 Cup
Vanilla Extract 1.0 Teapsoon
Baking Powder 0.5 Teaspoon
Salt 0.5 Teaspoon
Walnut Halves 0.5 Cup

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees. Grease a 9x13-inch pan.

  2. Mix sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla, baking powder and salt in a large bowl until combined. Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Decorate with walnut halves.

  3. Bake in preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull awat from the sides of the pan. About 20-30 minutes of cooling before slicing into squares.
